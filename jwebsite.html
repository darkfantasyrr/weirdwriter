What is is about:

This app is about blogging you playing an instrument and getting helpful feedback from your viewers. This app is helpful both directions. This is because the person playing will get helpful feedback from the viewer that will help them improve while the viewer gets to listen to music.This is why the app is helpful both directions

What makes my website easy:

My website also makes finding a piece easy. In the search bar, you put in what intrument you want to listen to and what instrument is the player playing and it narrows down the search by a lot. On the home page, it also shows what you watched recently, the video you made and what video you probably want to watch. In conclusion, even if you don't know what to listen to, the website will make finding/choosing a piece easy.
